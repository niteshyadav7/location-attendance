1. Information Collection and Use

The Application collects and uses the following information to provide and improve the Service:

a. Personal Information

We collect personally identifiable information such as your Name and Email Address. This data is used for Account Management, Authentication, and to identify your attendance records within your organization.

b. Location Data

Precise Location Data (GPS): The Application collects your device's precise location data only when the app is in use (foreground).
Purpose: This data is strictly used to verify your presence at designated job sites during "Check-In" and "Check-Out" actions. Location data is stored in our secure database as part of your attendance history to prevent fraud and ensure accurate timekeeping.
Background Location: This app DOES NOT track your location in the background or when the app is closed.

We collect Device IDs and other unique identifiers to:
