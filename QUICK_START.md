## 📱 **Test It Now!**

### **Step 1: Build the App**
```bash
npx react-native run-android
```

### **Step 2: Login as Admin**
- Open the app
- Login with admin credentials
- Keep app running (can minimize)

### **Step 3: Test with User**
- On another device, login as regular user
- Perform check-in
- **Admin device shows notification!** 🔔

---

## ⚠️ **One Requirement**

**Admin app must be running** (foreground or background)

- ✅ Works when app is open
- ✅ Works when app is minimized
- ❌ Won't work if app is force-closed

**This is normal** - it's the trade-off for avoiding Cloud Functions!
